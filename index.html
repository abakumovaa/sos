    Windows 7 64-bit only

    Intel Core 2 Duo @ 2.4 GHZ or AMD Athlon X2 @ 2.8 GHZ

  • MEMORY:

    8 GB RAM

  • STORAGE:

    8 GB available space

  • GRAPHICS:

    NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 2GB or
    AMD Radeon HD 7850 2GB DirectX11 (Shader Model 5)
